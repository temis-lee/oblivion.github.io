Full Episode Synopsis

The series premieres with Dr. Elara Vex, a brilliant but reclusive astrobiologist working at the Lunar Deep Space Array. While reviewing routine data, she stumbles upon an anomalous signal originating from Proxima Centauri b. The signal defies all known natural phenomena - its mathematical structure suggests artificial origin.

As Elara investigates further, the signal grows stronger and more complex. Strange occurrences begin worldwide: electronics malfunction in synchronized patterns, migratory animals change course simultaneously, and people across different continents report identical vivid dreams of a "great awakening."

The scientific community is divided - some believe it's a hoax, others think it's natural phenomena, but Elara becomes convinced it's an intentional message. When she discovers the signal contains what appears to be a countdown embedded in prime numbers, she races against time to decipher its meaning before the mysterious transmission completes its sequence.

The episode ends with a shocking revelation - the signal isn't just coming from Proxima Centauri b. It's being relayed from somewhere much farther away... and much older.
